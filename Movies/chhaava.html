Description

Chhaava tells the epic story of Maratha warrior-king Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj,
son of Shivaji, as he defends his empire against the formidable Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb,
focusing on his nine-year struggle, valor, political challenges, and ultimate betrayal and torture,
portraying his fight for dharma, kingdom, and refusal to convert. The film, based on Shivaji
Sawant's novel, highlights his guerrilla tactics, strong bond with wife Yasubai, internal court
conspiracies, and the need for Indian unity,
culminating in his gruesome death but immortalizing him as a heroic figure.
