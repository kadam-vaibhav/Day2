Description

Dhurandhar tells the story of Hamza (Ranveer Singh), a mysterious Indian RAW agent sent to infiltrate the dangerous Lyari underworld in Karachi, Pakistan, to dismantle a terrorist network linked to Pakistan's ISI, rising through the ranks of gangster Rehman Dacait to expose the nexus from within, amidst high-stakes espionage, betrayals, and the fallout from real-life terror attacks like 26/11.
