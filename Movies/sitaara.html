Description

Sitaare Zameen Par (2025) tells the story of Gulshan Arora (Aamir Khan), a disgraced, cynical basketball coach sentenced to community service for drunk driving, who must train a basketball team of adults with intellectual disabilities. Initially resistant, he slowly transforms as he connects with his players, learning life lessons about empathy, true strength, and inclusion, realizing they are teaching him more than he's teaching them, culminating in a journey towards a national tournament and personal redemption.
