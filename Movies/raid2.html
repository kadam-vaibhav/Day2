Description

Raid 2 isn't a direct retelling of one single true story, but it's inspired by real income tax raids, particularly a major one in Uttar Pradesh involving a politician-businessman accused of massive tax fraud, blending real incidents with fictional elements to create the sequel's plot. Director Raj Kumar Gupta aimed for a mix of reality and fiction, unlike the first Raid, which was based more closely on India's longest-ever income tax raid.
