Description

Mahavatar Narsimha is a visually stunning 2025 animated mythological film, part of the larger "Mahavatar Cinematic Universe," retelling the story of Lord Vishnu's fierce, half-man/half-lion avatar, Narasimha, who appears to save his devotee Prahlada from his tyrannical father, Hiranyakashipu, embodying divine justice and the triumph of faith over evil through spectacular animation and deep spiritual emotion, notes The Times of India, this Facebook post, and this Times of India article.
